1. Purpose of Our Services

The Math Effect Tutoring offers supplementary academic support to assist students in mastering content, practicing problems, and preparing for assessments responsibly. Tutorials complement official UJ sessions and should not replace them.

2. Academic Integrity

  • No plagiarism or copying
  • Do not submit tutor content as your own
  • Do not upload or share active UJ assessments
  • Never request tutors to complete assessments

Violators will be removed and reported.

3. Student Responsibility

  • Use lessons only for learning
  • Do your own work
  • Attend all UJ classes
  • Keep academic integrity

4. WhatsApp Group & Lesson Conduct

Allowed: Respectful questions, academic sharing, helping others

Prohibited: Bullying, discrimination, spamming, offensive content

5. Tutor Conduct

All tutors are expected to be respectful, inclusive, avoid favoritism, and use a student-centered teaching style. Tutors will not answer live assessments.

6. Confidentiality

All student information is private. Group chats are monitored and issues are handled professionally.

7. Fees & Refunds

  • Full payment required before session starts
  • No refunds for cancellations under 6 hours

8. Disclaimer

The Math Effect is not affiliated with UJ. No grade outcomes are guaranteed.
